About

I’m a Business & Functional Analyst with over 10 years of experience across retail, supply chain and logistics. My background combines analytical rigor with hands-on delivery: from requirements gathering and functional specifications to user journeys, testing coordination and rollout.

I’ve worked in enterprise environments, often in ERP and data-heavy contexts, translating operational needs into clear, testable solutions. I’m used to collaborating with business stakeholders, developers, testers and vendors across both Agile and more structured delivery models.

In parallel with my current role, I’m building AI applications hands-on — including LLM-based workflows, agents and orchestration patterns — with a strong focus on structure and reliability. I’m particularly interested in how AI can support documentation, validation, structured extraction and decision support without sacrificing traceability.

My approach remains pragmatic: clear scope, measurable outcomes, and solutions that can actually be maintained.
