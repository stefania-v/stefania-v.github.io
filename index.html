About

My background sits at the intersection of business and delivery. I’ve worked on enterprise projects in retail and logistics, often in ERP and data-heavy contexts, translating operational needs into functional specifications, user journeys and testable requirements.

I’m comfortable collaborating across teams (business stakeholders, developers, testers and vendors) and across methodologies (Agile and more structured delivery). I like clarity: well-defined scope, transparent assumptions, and solutions that are maintainable.

Recently, I’ve been focusing on AI applications that improve delivery and decision-making: agentic workflows, orchestration patterns, structured extraction and summarization — always paired with validation and evaluation so outputs can be trusted in real processes.

I’m building these projects hands-on (code + deployment) and documenting them like I would in a real product setting: requirements, edge cases, quality checks, and measurable outcomes.
